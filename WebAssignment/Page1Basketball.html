Basketball

The first interest I'd like to talk about is Basketball. Basketball was the sport I would gravitate towards in the school yards during recess. It also helped gain friends. Sometime during middleschool I decided to take basketball more seriously and improve my skills. I ended up joining the Tompkins Square Middle School basketball team but didn't get to play much due to poor grades. When I got to highschool I joined the Junior Varsity team and soon after joined the Varsity team. Those times were some of the best times of my life. I had a coach who taught me the fundamentals of basketball and because I was a skilled player I earned a lot of playing time. Being a good basketball player also got me a lot of respect from my peers
