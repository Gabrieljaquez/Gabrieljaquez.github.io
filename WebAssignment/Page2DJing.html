DJing

The second interest I'd like to talk about is DJing. I was first introduced to DJing when I would visit my father at his house. He would have a few DJ controllers and some speakers. I was so eager to learn how to mix songs. When I first started out my favorite function of the DJ controller was the Hot Cue function. This function allowed you to pick a point of a song and let you control it by the press of a button. Everytime you'd press the button it would jump to that part of the song. The reason why I like DJing so much is because it allows me to express myself musically without having to create my own song. I plan on learning how to make electronic music in the future. This would put my graphic design skills to use because I have a lot of images that I can use as album covers.
